What is Redbubble?

Redbubble is a print-on-demand marketplace. Artists upload their designs to Redbubble, and people are able to purchase merchandise with that design printed on it. Artists are then paid a certain percent of each sale.

Products

Some of the products that Redbubble offers are:

  • Stationery – cards, notebooks, stickers, ...
  • Home Decor – posters, canvases, pillows, ...
  • Drinkware – coffee mugs, travel mugs, and water bottles
  • Accessories – backpacks, totebags, pins, ...
  • Apparel – shirts, hoodies, socks, ...
  • Phone, tablet, and laptop cases
    • And More!

Because I only want to list products that work well with each design, not all products are availible for every design. That being said, Redbubble offers a large range of products, so that still leaves many options for you to choose from.
